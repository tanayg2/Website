Visualizing Altitudes and Successes of Rocket Launches with Dogs

I chose to visualize the altitudes that the rocket launches in this dataset were able to reach. I visualized the different types of rockets using a color scale. The marks used were rockets defined by svg's. The channels are the color of the rockets. Rocket launch dates were used as categorical variables and plotted on the x axis
